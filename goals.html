Quality Policy

Quality Objectives

ASCOT strongly adheres to the Philippine education sector's overarching goal of "Ensured lifelong learning opportunities for all", through the following quality objectives:
-Ensure relevant and quality tertiary education to achieve inclusive growth;
-Improve higher education research and innovation to promote sustainable development;
-Improve extension services to empower communities; and
-Increase productivity through proactive entrepreneurial projects.

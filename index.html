MANDATE OF THE COLLEGE
The State College shall primarily provide technical and professional training
in the sciences, arts, teacher education, agriculture, engineering, and technology
as well as short-term vocational courses. It shall likewise promote research,
advanced studies, and academic leadership in the stated area
of specialization. (Section 2 of RA 7664)
